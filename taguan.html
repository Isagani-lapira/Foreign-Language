Activity No.1

Try now to practice Hanzi character writing. First, watch the video on how to properly write the characters for the Pinyin "Nǐ hǎo". Then, practice writing the characters based on what you have learned from the video.



Activity No.2

LISTENING and SPEAKING Activity. Listen to the given audio files (FL 310 Class Site). Practice the correct pronunciation of the Mandarin phases and record your own audio file reciting the five phrases.




Activity No.3

Get a partner from your FL 301 classmates. Watch the video on "How to Introduce Yourself in Mandarin?" on FL 301 Class Site. Based on the video and UNIT 1 lessons that you have learned, do your virtual conversation through any virtual meeting.



Activity No.4

Record a short video wherein you are going to show a picture of your best friend. Introduce your best friend and tell us something about him/her. Also, share with us what ball game is he/she interested to play and/or what language does he/she is interested to study.


Activity No.5

Using a piece of bond paper and a board marker, write the Hanzi characters for "Happy birthday!". Record a video while you are writing the Hanzi characters and show your output. Greet your friend or relative (in Mandarin) who will be celebrating his/her birthday this October to December.

Activity No.6

Using Canva templates, write your own short diary with 10-15 Mandarin sentences (Pinyin). You may omit the tone symbols for this activity. Be creative and artistic with your output.



Activity No.7

Get a partner from your classmates. Do your virtual conversation. Be sure to record your conversation using screen recording. One of you will act as the CUSTOMER and the other one will be the Mc Donald's CREW.
